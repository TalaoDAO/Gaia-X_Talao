This web application is an example of a Gaia-X onboarding process for enterprise wallets.
It is based on the up-to-date protocols and signature schemes in respect of the W3C and DIF standards.



1 - Download the Talao smartphone wallet from the Google or Apple store
2 - Setup your wallet as an enterprise wallet with the key provided by your company or check the repository to get our demo key and DID
3 - Start your journey : Get a Gaia-X Pass or go directly to the Sign-In page.
